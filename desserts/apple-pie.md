# Apple pie

## Ingredients

- **4** apples
- **1 glass** of flour
- **1 glass** of farina
- **100 g** of sugar
- **teaspoon** of baking powder
- **butter**

## Instructions

1. Grate the apples
2. Mix the dry ingreedients
3. Put on a baking tray layers of apples inbetween layers of dry ingreedients
4. Slice butter and cover the last layer
5. Bake in 200 degrees for 25 min
6. Serve with a scoop of ice cream
